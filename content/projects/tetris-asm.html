Controls

Left: left arrow

Right: right arrow

Soft Drop: down arrow

Rotate Clockwise: up arrow

Rotate Counter-Clockwise: Z / Ctrl

Rotate 180°: A

Hold: shift


Specs

DAS: 0.13s

ARR: 0.02s

Fall Speed: line / 0.8s

Soft Drop: fall speed × 50
